### 📍 LocalLens *(flagship — in active development)*

> **Community-powered local discovery.** See your city through the eyes of real people — not algorithms.

```
🗺️  Interactive live map     →  geo-tagged posts from real users
📸  Photo moments            →  upload & pin experiences to locations  
🔥  Trending discovery       →  surface what's hot near you right now
👤  Community profiles       →  follow locals, build your map footprint
🏷️  Smart categories         →  Food · Urban · Sunset · Wildlife · +more
📊  500+ spots · 15K+ views · 200+ users (organic, no ads)
```

**Stack:** `React` `Laravel` `Inertia.js` `Tailwind` `Leaflet Maps` `PostGIS` `MySQL`
